About Zestfestian

Zestfestian is a language made by 7 kids from South-East England. It's not meant to be spoken, but actually meant to be typed. Below is the basic info you'll need to write in Zestfestian.

How do you speak Zestfestian?

Zestfestian is a language that’s alphabet is made up of ZzEeSsTtFfE’e’S’s’T’t’Z’z’E’’e’’S’’ as the consonants and the vowels being L and then the corresponding vowel (e.g La, Lo).

This means the alphabet is:

la, Z, z, E, le, e, S, s, li, T, t, F, f. E’, lo, e’, S’, s’, T’, t’, lu, Z’, z’, E’’, e’’, S’’.

All letters of the alphabet correspond to their English counterpart in the Latin alphabet.
